About Dr. Asha Patel

Dr. Asha Patel is a highly respected and board-certified cardiologist with over 15 years of experience in diagnosing and treating cardiovascular conditions. Her clinical expertise includes coronary artery disease, heart failure, hypertension, and preventive cardiology.
